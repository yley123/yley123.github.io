About

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Manchester, working on neural rendering and computer graphics. My research focuses on developing physics-informed approaches for material synthesis and video generation, with particular interests in SVBRDF generation, neural materials, and diffusion models. I have published at top venues including EGSR and Computer Graphics Forum.
